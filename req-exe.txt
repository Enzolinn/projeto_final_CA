Criar um todo
curl -s -X POST http://localhost:5000/todos \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"title":"Comprar cimento","done":false}' | jq


Listar todos
curl -s http://localhost:5000/todos | jq

Pegar por id
curl -s http://localhost:5000/todos/1 | jq

Atualizar
curl -s -X PUT http://localhost:5000/todos/1 \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"done":true}' | jq

Deletar
curl -s -X DELETE http://localhost:5000/todos/1 | jq
